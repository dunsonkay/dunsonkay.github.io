What We Do

CREATIVE SERVICES FOR BRANDS, EVENTS, AND EXPERIENCES.

Curated by KK offers full-service creative support for people, brands, and businesses who want their visuals, events, and brand moments to feel intentional, elevated, and unforgettable.

Service Description Includes
01 Content Creation We help bring your brand story to life through creative visuals that feel authentic, polished, and aligned with your purpose.
  • Creative direction and mood board development
  • Social media content planning
  • Photo and video shoot concepts
  • Brand storytelling and visual styling
  • Short-form promotional content
02 Event Styling We design and style events with thoughtful details that create a specific mood, message, and experience.
  • Event theme and concept development
  • Decor direction and styling
  • Tablescape and visual setup planning
  • Color palette and design details
  • Day-of styling support
03 Brand Experiences We create memorable experiences that connect your audience to your brand through intentional visuals and storytelling.
  • Pop-up concepts and creative activations
  • Launch event experience planning
  • Interactive brand moments
  • Visual storytelling for live experiences
  • Guest experience direction
04 Creative Direction We help shape ideas into a cohesive visual concept with clear direction and intentional design.
  • Concept development
  • Visual direction and brand alignment
  • Shot list and content planning
  • Styling guidance
  • Creative consulting
